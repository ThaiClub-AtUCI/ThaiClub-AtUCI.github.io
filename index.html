About the Club

Welcome to Thai Club at UC Irvine! We are a diverse group of students, all from different nationalities, who have an interest in Thai culture and community service.

Our ultimate goal is to form a tightly knit group of friends and family, while being able to share our Thai culture with our peers, friends, family, professors, etc of our campus. We hope that you will support us, and please keep checking back to see if we have updates or events coming up!

Events

  • Thai Club at UC Irvine brings members together by holding activities like weekly general meetings and fundraisers.

    We celebrate the holidays together by hosting potlucks and kickbacks like our Thanksgiving and ValenThai parties.

    At the end of the year, we also host our annual banquet and spring retreat as a reward for our members for helping out throughout the year.

  • General Meetings

    Thai Club at UCI have weekly general meeting to discuss upcoming events and volunteer opportunities. During the meeting, the officers will also educate club members about Thai culture we also teach simple Thai vocabulary to our members.

  • Pad Thai/ Pad See Ew Fundraiser

    Our main fundraising event is selling Pad Thai, Pad-See-Ew, Thai tea and Pink Milk(Nhom yen) to simultaneously raise money and promote interest in Thai cooking.

  • Irvine Global Village Festival

    The Irvine Thai Arts and Culture group, in collaboration with the Thai Association of Southern California put together the Thailand Exhibit booth at IGVF. We had showcase a short dance performance at the booth and provide volunteers from our club to help out at the booth as well.

  • ITSA Bonfire

    Thai student organizations from other schools in Southern California join us for an annual bonfire to get together and expand our social network as Thai students.

  • Scavenger Hunt at Downtown Disney

    The members participate in a photo scavenger hunt around downtown Disney and enjoyed a dinner together as a group.

  • Bowling Night

    The members get together for a night of bowling.

  • ITSA Sports Day

    The Intercollegiate Thai Association comes together and plan various activities such as chair ball, three legged race, and many more.

  • Thai New Year Festival in Thai Town

    Thai Club at UCI is given a volunteering opportunity to perform Thai traditional dance at the Thai New Year Songkran Festival in Hollywood and to be part of the parade.

  • Retreat at Big Bear

    As a thank you for the members, we go out to Big Bear and bond through days and nights of group activities.

  • End of the Year Banquet

    This is the very last event hosted by Thai Club at UCI to appreciate the members for all of their hard work and cooperation throughout the year. It will also be an act of congratulating our graduating seniors or students who are leaving. It will mainly be a fun, semi-formal event to gather all of our members and friends. It will be a celebration and a night of reflection.
