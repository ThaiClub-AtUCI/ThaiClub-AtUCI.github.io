About the Club

Welcome to Thai Club at UC Irvine! We are a diverse group of students, all from different nationalities, who have an interest in Thai culture and community service.

Our ultimate goal is to form a tightly knit group of friends and family, while being able to share our Thai culture with our peers, friends, family, professors, etc of our campus. We hope that you will support us, and please keep checking back to see if we have updates or events coming up!

    2015 - The Foregoing Flower

    In the year 1815, Soi, a talented young woman is happily in love with Sing. Little did she know that the sister of her best friend, Gumlai, is infatuated with him. With strong jealousy, Gumlai and the evil Witch Doctor put a curse to trap Soi in a magical flower in the woods and the only way to break the curse is through the descendant of Gumlai’s brother two hundred years after. What brings him to the woods in the first place? What obstacle stands in his way?

    2014 - Love at First Punch

    Adventures in Thailand, here we go! Let’s follow Eric, Thai-American as he travels into the countryside of Thailand, searching for the rumored master of Muay Thai residing there. What happens though when the Master isn’t who he expected it to be? With no phone service, how will they find the way back to the city on their own? With this clash between him and the master, will his training go successfully or will his dreams fall the pieces in ruin? Let’s take a look at his time in the village with Songkran and the annual Muay Thai competition fast approaching. Who knows? He may learn more than just Muay Thai.

    2013 - Tavipop: Siam Renaissance

    The play revolves around an antique mirror that joins the two worlds of the past and present together. Manee, the main character and a modern girl, travels through the antique mirror and discovers an adventure of a lifetime - saving Siam and meeting her destiny, Khun Luang Akkarathep Warakorn, the Minister of Foreign Affairs in 1890. Manee must decide where her heart truly belongs in this mystic play of fantasy and romance.
